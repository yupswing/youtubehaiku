You Tube Haiku.net

Enjoy the videos posted by the users of the subreddit r/youtubehaiku in a nice modern player that will make you waste even more time than usual.

You just need to know that HAIKU are poetic videos under 14 seconds, POETRY are still poetic but longer (under 30 seconds) and then, you know, there are Memes .

youtubehaiku.net is made by Simone Cingano (aka u/yupswing).
The project is open source and you can fork the code on github

Play!

If you have comments or ideas contact me .

Control the player with the buttons or use the keyboard shortcuts :

  • Double click/tap on the video to toggle fullscreen
  • or S or . to open the channel selection
  • or C or L to open the reddit post
  • or → or N to skip to the next video
  • or P or SPACE to play/pause
  • or R to replay the current video
  • or ← or B to go back to the previous video
  • or ↑ or ↓ or M to change the volume
  • or R to replay the current video
  • or H or . to show this help box again
